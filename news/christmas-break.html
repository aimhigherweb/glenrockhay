christmas break

13/11/2014

0 Comments

 
Well the year is drawing to a close very quickly and November is almost half over.  Christmas will be upon us before we know it .
Our final delivery for 2014 will be Monday 22/12 and we will resume deliveries the week of 12/1/2015.

Please start to look ahead and see if your regular delivery falls during our break.  This will depend on whether you get a fortnightly or 4 weekly delivery.  For some of our customers, our Christmas break will not affect you at all, but others will skip a delivery.

Please start to plan ahead for your feed requirements so we can endeavour to get to everyone before the holidays with enough hay to see you through our break.

If you are unsure of any of this, just drop us a quick email and we’ll answer any questions.
